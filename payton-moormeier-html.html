Payton Moormeier - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Friday, March 1, 2024

Social star best known for his payton TikTok account, which has earned over 15 million fans. He posts comedic lip syncs and dance videos, often alongside such friends as Anthony Reeves, Jaden Hossler and Avani Gregg. Also a singer, he released his first single "Love Letter" in April 2020. His self-titled YouTube channel is devoted to his music and features multiple official music videos for singles of his such as "Hard to Breathe" and "Habits." 

Before Fame

He got verified on TikTok on April 29, 2017. He was a member of the TikTok group JrElite until his departure in the summer of 2017.

Trivia

He attended Playlist Live in Orlando in 2018. He featured Anthony Reeves in a 2019 TikTok video captioned "Anthony's teaching me his ways" that has earned over 1 million likes. In July 2019, he competed against Jaden Hossler and Tayson Madkour in a Wall of Fame challenge. 

Family Life

His mother's name is Joanne. He grew up alongside his sister Faith in St Marys, Georgia. He dated Cierra Birdsong.

Associated With

He has posted TikTok duets with fellow social star King Tino.
