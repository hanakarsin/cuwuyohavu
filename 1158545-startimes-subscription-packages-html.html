StarTimes subscription packages, prices and channels 2024

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Wednesday, March 13, 2024

StarTimes is a subscription-based television service that has revolutionized the Pay Television industry in Nigeria. StarTimes Nigeria provides televised content via a Digital Terrestrial Television (DTT) Network service that supports both the Terrestrial TV (DTT) Service and Satellite TV (DTH). What are the different StarTimes packages in 2024, and how much do they cost? Chinedu Lazarus Ibeabuchi, the senior marketing communications and public relations (PR) manager at StarTimes Nigeria, provides insights into the latest bouquet prices and new offers the cable TV service introduced.

TABLE OF CONTENTS

The company serves its customers through antenna signal transmission (DTT) and satellite transmission (DTH). The packages under the two transmission modes vary slightly.

Difference between StarTimes decoders

During a discussion with Chinedu Lazarus Ibeabuchi, the senior marketing communications and public relations (PR) manager at StarTimes Nigeria, he shed light on the distinctions among StarTimes decoders. According to him, the antenna transmission works like a digital terrestrial television (DTT), while the dish (satellite) transmission operates as a direct-to-home (DTT) system.

StarTimes offers antenna and dish Pay-TV packages to meet different households' entertainment needs and financial abilities. The pricier a package is, the more channels you can view. You can change packages whenever you please. Our DTH decoder transmits more channels, and many channels are in HD. StarTimes DTT has about 100 TV channels, while the DTH has over 130 channels (100+ TV channels and 20+ audio channels). The DTT decoder is N9900, while the DTH decoder is N13,500 for a new one.

Ibeabuchi also mentioned that the StarTimes Special bouquet (which comes with the DTH decoder) costs ₦5,000 per month.

How much do StarTimes decoders, antennae, and dishes cost?

Here is how much you will spend on the various combos.

  • DTH decoder, dish, accessories, and 1-month Super bouquet subscription: ₦13,200
  • DTH decoder, dish, accessories, and 1-month Smart bouquet subscription: ₦11,200
  • DTT decoder, antenna, and 1-month Classic bouquet subscription: ₦9,400
  • DTT decoder and 1-month Classic bouquet subscription: ₦7,600

There are numerous StarTimes packages for prospective customers to choose from. These vary in the signal reception technology, prices, and channel list. With the different pricing options, one can easily find a package that fits their budget and has sufficient content in terms of the available channels.
