Burna Boy's bae Stefflon Don to send N2.2m to treat Nigerian man's sick mother

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Monday, January 1, 0001

- Rapper Burna Boy's girlfriend has provided help to a Nigerian man whose mother is sick

- The British rapper Stefflon Don promised to send N2.2 million (£5k) to the man to treat his mother

- Don made the promise after she hit one billion streams, and asked people to make wishes

Popular Nigerian rapper Burna Boy's girlfriend Stefflon Don has shown love to her lover's country people. The British rapper promised to give a sum of N2.2 million (£5k) to a Nigerian man.

The rapper made the move after her song hit one billion streams. She took to Twitter to thank her fans and offered to do a cash giveaway.

A Nigerian man identified as Shocka Artist used the opportunity to call on the rapper for help. He revealed that his mother has cancer, and she has been told that she only has months to live.
