Massawe Japanni Celebrates Hubby in Sweet Message: &quot;I Treasure Every Moment with You&quot;

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Saturday, February 17, 2024
  • Journalists Massawe and Tom Japanni are blessed with three adorable daughters
  • The bubbly Radio Jambo presenter is a darling to many, thanks to her sweet voice and prowess on the mic
  • In May 2021, the couple celebrated their 14th marriage anniversary
  • Massawe is known for going all out when celebrating her husband's and their children's birthdays
  • The mother of three showered her better half with love on his 51st birthday

Maureen Massawe and her husband Tom Japanni are a great couple. The two are a perfect match made in heaven.

Massawe and Japanni are among the power couples working in the same profession. The couple is one of the most loved in the showbiz industry just like Nameless and Wahu and Lulu and Rashid.

They rarely thrive on controversy as some of the couples in the limelight.

Japanni turned 51 years old, and celebrating him on his special day, Massawe showered the father of her three children with love.

"Life is so precious and should be treasured. I treasure every moment with you and I’m so grateful for another year to spend with you. Wishing you a happy birthday Tom Japanni," she wrote.
