Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Thursday, February 8, 2024
  • A baby has shown that he preferred his mother to his father as he chose the former many times in a family game
  • Despite the man's repeated calls for his attention, the kid ran to the mother's warm embrace in a way that stirred laughter
