Romantic Man Gifts Girlfriend Amazing Mirror Displaying Bible Verses

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Sunday, April 21, 2024

- A young romantic man has proven the meaning of true love after he gifted his girlfriend a beautiful, smart mirror

- To ensure that his girlfriend does not fall short of forgetting important things in her life, he programmed a to-do-list function in the mirror

- Netizens showered him with praises, with many saying that he went out of his way to prove his love to his girlfriend in a unique way

A young Nigerian man by the Twitter handle, @TadelayoS, is full of surprises after doing something extraordinary for his girlfriend.

The man revealed that he developed a smart mirror for his girlfriend that can display Bible scriptures and remind her how beautiful she is.

Additionally, the unique mirror can also remind the lady what she has on her to-do list for each day. In a subsequent post, the man shared other photos showing different angles of the mirror.
