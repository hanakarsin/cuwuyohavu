Father Mbaka Reveals The Most Peaceful Governor In Nigeria

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Thursday, February 15, 2024

Rev. Fr. Ejike Mbaka, has on Tuesday revealed that the governor of Enugu, Ifeanyi Ugwuanyi is the most peaceful governor in the country.

Mbaka, said this during a courtesy visit to the governor at the Government House, Enugu.

The Spiritual Director who led a delegation of Red Star Belgrade Football Club, Serbia and Antalyaspor Football Club, Turkey to visit the governor said that peace is in the Governor’s nature and “peace is in his integrity”.

The clergy man told the governor that the delegation were football scouts from Turkey and Serbia who were planning to use Enugu State for their scouting activity”,

According to him, “Among all the eastern governors, you (Ugwuanyi) are number one who don’t have any controversy. We respect you and respect Enugu State government. Here is a peaceful place, there is no problem of security here and the people of Enugu State love you”.

Responding, Ugwuanyi welcomed the delegation and assured them of the state’s support toward their endeavors.

“If our people are discovered, we will benefit a lot”, he said.
