'Junior Pope Used Me' - Nollywood Actress Esther Gives Details Of S3x Escapade

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Sunday, July 28, 2024

Fast-rising Nollywood actress, Esther Nwachukwu, has opened up about her romantic relationship with her senior colleague, Junior Pope.

Naija News reports that Esther, in a chat with an Instagram blogger, accused the actor of using her to chase clout and also revealed how they both agreed to stage fake content online.

The actress said she had earlier tattooed the initials ‘JP’, which means ‘Just Peace’. However, Junior Pope advised her to lie online that the tattoo was for him in order to trend on social media.

Sadly, the movie star didn’t keep to their agreement, instead, he made a mockery of her online and later begged for forgiveness after the incident faded online.

Esther also revealed that Junior Pope asked her to chase clout with his wife by unfollowing her on Instagram, which was planned, and she would be paid for it.

However, the actor failed to keep to his words and subjected her to ridicule online by denying her.

Esther noted that in the wake of the incident, some people reported her Instagram page, and it was disabled.

The actress also alleged that they were also involved in a romantic relationship with  Junior Pope, always requesting money and never paid back.
