15 Best online shopping sites and stores in Kenya in 2024

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Saturday, April 6, 2024

As more people go digital, sellers are taking advantage of the opportunity to offer their products and services to a larger Kenyan market. This opportunity has enabled people to shop whenever it is convenient for them. Check out this list of Kenyan online shops.

Kenyans are well-known for their enthusiasm for all things technological and innovative. The number of Kenyans who prefer to buy things online has rapidly increased in recent years and will continue to rise. Are you out of ideas on where to buy things online in Kenya? Check out these stores where you can purchase anything.

Top 15 online shops in Kenya

Don't want to waste your valuable time shopping in ordinary stores and instead start shopping in online stores in Kenya? Discover the top 15 affordable shops online where you can buy anything from clothing, shoes, and electronics to ballpoint pens with digital watches.

1. Jumia

Which app is best for online shopping in Kenya? Jumia is one of the best Kenya's largest online shopping site. You can buy mobile phones, tablets, computers and laptops, women's and men's clothing, food, and other items and have them delivered to you.

In addition, it allows businesses to sell items on its platform. Jumia also accepts all forms of payment, including cash, mobile money, and visa and credit cards. They deliver products to homes or pick them up at various locations across the country and charge based on the size and weight of the items.

2. Carrefour

Carrefour is another large Kenya online market store where buyers and sellers can make a variety of commodities. The site is more dependable because you can pay upon delivery. They have exclusive deals that will save you a lot of money.

3. Ovostore Kenya

Looking for a great place to buy your phone? Ovostore Kenya is one of the leading online electronics shopping sites in Kenya that brings you great deals.

They offer unbeatable deals featuring an endless range of electronic products priced at affordable rates, including mobile phones, tablets, appliances, and much more. Additionally, they provide cash on-delivery service countrywide.

4. Kilimall

Want to buy more while spending less? Then Kilimall is the place for you! It is one of the most affordable shopping platforms in Kenya.

This shop operates similarly to Jumia, where entrepreneurs set up shops to sell their wares as long as they are genuine and brand new.

Phones and accessories, clothing, shoes, office supplies, beauty and hair products, and many other items are available. They also make home deliveries in Nairobi and use posta deliveries to other parts of the country.

5. Jiji

Jiji sells a wide variety of products, including food, electronics, and automobiles. This particular website allows you to buy and sell used items.

Another advantage of Jiji is that you can look for houses and apartments to rent or buy. Buyers who cannot afford the prices associated with brand-new items also benefit from this site.

6. Masoko

Masoko is yet another Kenya's best online stores for purchasing mobile phones and other electronics. It is managed and operated by Safaricom, Kenya's leading telecommunications provider.

You are guaranteed quality products on the platform, and it even accepts money-back and instant returns on unsatisfactory products upon delivery. Despite their slightly higher prices, it is one of the best websites for getting genuine items.

7. Sky Garden

Sky Garden is a retail site that enables you to buy or sell online. Registration and product uploading are both free and easy. If you happen to be in Nairobi, your orders are delivered within 24 hours, while orders outside of Nairobi are delivered within 2 to 3 days. You can pay with a Visa or an MPesa card.

8. Shopit

Shopit is another convenient store specialising primarily in various electronic products. It offers a wide range of high-quality, leading-brand products, including mobile phones, laptops, desktop computers, netbooks, tablets, and LCD televisions.

9. Autocheck

Do you want to buy a new car? Then you should go to Autocheck, Kenya's largest online car-selling platform. Any car can be bought, sold, or imported here.

They have partnered with major car distributors to bring you great affordable, brand-new vehicles. The platform provides all the information about a specific car, such as the vehicle type, price, year of manufacture, and mileage.

10. MamaMikes

MamaMikes is dedicated to assisting Kenyans living abroad to stay in touch with their loved ones back home. You can shop for various items such as airtime, shopping for your family, romantic hampers, ruracio items, and much more. The best part is that you can place an order directly from your smartphone using WhatsApp.

11. Electrohub

Elecrohub primarily sells home electronics such as televisions, speakers, and projectors, as well as accessories such as earphones and headphones. They offer expert consultation, custom design, installation, and maintenance of a wide range of home and business technologies.

You can reach them via Whatsapp or visit their stores in Westlands at Sarit Center, Highway Mall on Uhuru Highway, and Karen at Waterfront Mall.

12. Copia

Copia has already taken over the online marketplace. They've made a name for themselves by delivering goods to Kenya's most remote locations. Copia's product categories include construction materials, farm tools and products, health, appliances, and beauty products.

13. Pigiame

This is one of Kenya's most secure online marketplaces, conveniently connecting buyers and sellers. They cater for various interests, including electronics, home and gardening, children, vehicles and car parts, and real estate.

14. Jambo Shoppe

Jambo Shoppe, like Jumia, sells everything you can think of, from trendy electronics like laptops and smartphones to in-vogue fashion staples. They also deal with washing machines, televisions, air conditioners, blenders, and other time-saving kitchen and small appliances.

15. AliExpress

AliExpress is a Chinese online retail service owned by the Alibaba Group. This store is available in several countries, including Kenya. You can import anything you want at a lower cost than buying it locally. The majority of their items are of high quality and one-of-a-kind. The platform is ideal for those who run a business and enjoy making impulse purchases.

Which online store is the cheapest?

Kilimall is regarded as one of the cheapest online stores where you can buy anything and have it delivered to you anywhere in the country. They sell a wide range of products, making it easier to shop.

What are people buying online in Kenya?

With most people's busy schedules, most prefer online shopping, which sometimes saves them time and money. Kenyans buy a variety of items, including home appliances and furniture, beauty and fashion items, any type of electronic item, health products, home shopping and groceries, and many others.
