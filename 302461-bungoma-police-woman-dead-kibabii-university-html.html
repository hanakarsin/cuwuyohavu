Bungoma: Police woman found dead inside Kibabii University

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Friday, May 31, 2024

Detectives are investigating the mysterious death of a female police officer who allegedly collapsed and died within Kibabii University.

The body of Susan Njambi who until her death was attached to Kenya Institute of Curriculum Development was found lying lifeless on a pavement by her colleagues at around 6am on the dawn of Friday, April 5.

According to a police OB report, Njambi had lit a mosquito coil inside a locked motor vehicle on that fateful night.

She later alighted from the said van and sat on a pavement where she was discovered dead.

Officers from the crimes unit visited the scene and took her body to Bungoma County Referral Hospital mortuary awaiting an autopsy.

Police collected mosquito coils, a bottle of water and tomato ring crisps from the car as investigation continues.

The university is playing host to thousands of students during the 2019 edition of Kenya National Drama and Film Festival which will come to a close on Tuesday, April 9.
