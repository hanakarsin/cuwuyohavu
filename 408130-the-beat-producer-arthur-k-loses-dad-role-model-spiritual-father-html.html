The Beat Producer Arthur K Loses Dad: &quot;Role Model, Spiritual Father&quot;

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Friday, May 17, 2024

- Arthur announced his father's passing through his official Instagram account

- The Beat producer did not disclose the cause of his dad's death but noted he will miss him dearly

- His announcement hurt many people including local celebrities who thronged the comments section of his post to condole with him and his family

If you are an ardent music fan who is keen on Kenyan music, then music show, The Beat is something you must be familiar with.

Well, the music show which used to air in the evening was the creative works of a team led by producer Arthur K alias Mopao.

Arthur and his family are in mourning following the sudden demise of his father, Reverend Ngeru, TUKO.co.ke has learnt.

The sad news was first made public by Arthur K himself through his Instagram account.

In a post seen by TUKO.co.ke, the producer eulogised his dad and remembered him as his role model, a spiritual father.

Arthur also noted that his father was a very peaceful and calm man who loved God and everyone.

He did not disclose the cause of his father's death but noted he will really miss him.

''My Dad, Role model, Spiritual Father and a great community Leader. Peaceful, Calm and orderly Man of God, Rev Ngeru my father has Passed on to be with the Lord. I will miss you Dad. But I know you are in the place you preached about all your life. Most don’t know you taught me all that I know today and gave me all that I ever needed wanted or even desired. Until we meet again Dad Rest In Peace. Ephesians 4: 11-13'' Arthur captioned his dad's photo.
