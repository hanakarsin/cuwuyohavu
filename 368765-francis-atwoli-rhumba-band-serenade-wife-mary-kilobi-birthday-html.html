Francis Atwoli gets Rhumba band to serenade wife Mary Kilobi on her birthday

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Friday, June 14, 2024

- Mary Kilobi turned a year older on Thursday, July 16

- Her hubby Francis Atwoli marked her special day with a sweet post shared on his Facebook

- The day culminated in a very special treatment at their home where the flashy boss invited a band to serenade his wife

Birthdays are special days to most of us, and they get to be more exciting when people who are close to us do something out of the ordinary to make them memorable.

As news anchor Mary Kilobi turned a year older on Thursday, July 16, her hubby Francis Atwoli culminated the day with a special gift for her.
