Coyote Peterson's biography

Coyote Peterson was born on September 1, 1981, in Newbury, Ohio, United States. He celebrated his 40th birthday in September 2021. At birth, his parents named him Nathaniel Peterson. However, details about Coyote Peterson's family are not in the limelight.

Which schools did Coyote Peterson attend? He enrolled at Notre Dame-Cathedral Latin School. After graduating from the school, he went to Ohio State University to study video production, writing and directing. He graduated from the university in 2004.

His deep admiration for wildlife and video production inspired him to create a YouTube channel known as Brave Wilderness channel in September 2014.

Coyote has an adventurous nature and a joyful curiosity for wildlife. As a result, he can create a friendly and inviting space while getting up close with animals. The first video he uploaded on the channel about the snapping turtle garnered a lot of views within a short period of time.
