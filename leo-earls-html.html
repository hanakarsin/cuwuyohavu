Leo Earls - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Monday, April 8, 2024

Famous for being the first-born son of YouTube vloggers Rachel and Harold Earls. He was born in September 2017 as the couple braced for impact in Florida from Hurricane Irma. 

Before Fame

His parents rose to fame for vlogging about their experience as a military couple. His mother's pregnancy was announced to their YouTube channel in December 2016. 

Trivia

His family's YouTube channel has quickly amassed more than 500,000 subscribers. He has been frequently featured on his mother's rachelearls Instagram account. 

Family Life

In addition to his parents, his family has two dogs named Tracker and Ranger. He has younger brothers named Wyatt, Truett and Atticus.

Associated With

His father Harold Earls regularly operated the vlogging for his YouTube channel during his birth in September 2017.
