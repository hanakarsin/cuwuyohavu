Sadie Sandler - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Thursday, July 25, 2024

American actress and the daughter of actor Adam Sandler. She starred in Just Go with It in 2011 and That's My Boy in 2012.

Before Fame

She had her first role in the 2008 film Bedtime Stories.

Trivia

She appeared in the 2010 film Grown Ups.

Family Life

She was born to Adam Sandler and Jacqueline Titone and she has a younger sister named Sunny.

Associated With

She appeared alongside Jennifer Aniston in Just Go with It.
