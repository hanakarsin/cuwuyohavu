Ops1x - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Saturday, May 18, 2024

Twitch star and professional gamer who is best recognized for his ops1x Twitch channel, where he streams and posts Fortnite content. He has accumulated over 1.3 million subscribers on his Twitch channel. 

Before Fame

He began posting on YouTube in February 2020 and on his Twitch channel in September 2020. 

Trivia

He is a member of the ESports organization TSM. 

Family Life

His mother has appeared on his msf_ops Instagram account. 

Associated With

He and Twitch star Bjergsen are both members of TSM.
