Cristiano Ronaldo Receives Expensive Birthday Present From Girlfriend

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Thursday, July 25, 2024

International footballer Cristiano Ronaldo has received a Cadillac Escalade SUV for his 37th birthday from his partner Georgina Rodriguez.

The new car is a 4×4 which is no less than 5.5 metres in length and has an engine with up to 420 horsepower.

The Portuguese forward celebrated a new year on Saturday and Georgina who is currently pregnant celebrated his new age with him.

See the video she shared on Instagram.
