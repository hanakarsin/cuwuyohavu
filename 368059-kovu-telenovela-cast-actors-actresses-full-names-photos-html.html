Kovu telenovela cast: actors and actresses full names and photos

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Thursday, June 13, 2024

Kovu is a Swahili telenovela currently airing on Maisha Magic Plus. The show revolves around the mysteries that befall a wealthy family, the Hazes, which has recently moved from Tanzania to settle in Kenya. Trouble, bitterness, suffering, and endless drama become a new normal to the family when their long-kept secrets are revealed. Apart from its intriguing storyline, you may also want to know more about the Kovu Maisha Magic cast.

So far, who is your favourite character on the Kovu Maisha Magic East telenovela? Is it Rina, Carlos Santana, Madzu, Ruby, or Luka? Whoever it is, how much do you know about them? Most likely, it is little or none. Before exploring the cast, here is what you need to know about the show.

Who are the creators and director of Kovu?

Interestingly, Rashid Abdalla of Citizen TV is the creator and producer of this hot Swahili telenovela. Together with his wife, Lulu Hassan, they are the brains behind Maria, Aziza, Maza, Moyo, and Huba. Anthony Mbithi is the director of the telenovela.

Is Kovu on Showmax?

Yes. Apart from airing on Maisha Magic Plus, one can watch it on Showmax. At the moment, viewers can stream season one, which contains 78 episodes. Production of season two is also underway because two episodes are already available for streaming.

What time does Kovu air on Maisha Magic Plus?

Usually, the telenovela airs on Maisha Magic Plus from Monday to Friday – starting from 6:30 pm. What if you are not home by that time? Well, you can catch a repeat of the show, which runs on Sundays from 4 pm.

List of Kovu Maisha Magic actors and actresses

How is the cast like when they are not on set? Many may ask. Here is a list of the cast, their real names, and photos. These details will help you to understand them better – You will be shocked to learn that some are the complete opposite of what you see on the screens.

1. Carlos Santana – Patrick Owino

Santana, whose real name is Patrick Owino, is among the characters hated by many because of his strictness – overall, Satana is bad news. He lives in a huge lavish home together with a patient and a servant. Carlos is popularly known for his Godfather signature hat and a walking stick. He hardly takes orders from anyone.

Mr. Owino does not entirely depend on acting. According to his Instagram bio, he is a plant equipment manager at Seven Stars Ltd. Perhaps, now you understand why he is always time conscious.

2. Jaffar Haze – Kenneth Ambani

Kenneth Ambani is an award-winning actor. In the show, he (Jaffar Haze) is the man of the house and a husband to Alisa. Despite having all the wealth, Mr. Haze is a troubled man. His family has been torn apart by long-kept secrets that start to unravel once they relocate to Kenya.

Kenneth Ambani is also a marketing specialist, Communications and PR professional, and films and adverts director.

3. Alisa – Saada Fateh

Over the last few months, Saada Fateh has been the talk of the town because of her exemplary acting skills. She is the wife of Jaffar Haze. Her role is significant to the development of the telenovela. In real life, Saada Fateh is an exciting and lovely person to be around with. He is also a mother and super MC.

4. Rina – Sharlet Akinyi Mariam

In the show, she is the daughter of Jaffar and Alisa. To Alisa (mother), she is the scar that keeps tormenting her. Despite her sudden disappearance from the show, many are hoping to see her back soon.

Sharlet hails from Msambeni, Kwale County. Judging by his social media posts, she plans to contents in the upcoming elections. Akinyi is the founder of Hope Avenue, which has been offering help to the less fortunate in society.

5. Ruby – Trisha Khalid

Trisha Khalid is not only beautiful and intelligent but also extraordinarily talented. The 25-year-old is the owner of T Trendy Collection, a cloth line business. The shop is located in Bamburi Mtambo. In the show, her character is among those loved by many.

6. Roy Haze – Musa Kugotwah

In Kovu, he is the son of Jaffar and Alisa. He is a true definition of an entertainer. Apart from acting, Musa is also an audio developer. He is a regular guest of some of the country’s biggest music shows.

7. Luka – Rick Mutune

Mutune is another actor on the set with a considerable number of fans. He is a favourite to many ladies not only because of his talent, but also his body physique. Rick is also a model, singer, and writer.

8. Judy – Elizabeth Sonia Michael

Simply, Sonia is a jack of all trades. Despite being the best actress, she is also an experienced pharmacist, financial consultant, and television host. Elizabeth is also a mother to one lovely son, whose name is Prince Faiz. The Tanzanian actress also made a magical appearance on Maza series.

9. Madzu – John Maximiliano

John Maximiliano plays the role of a hilarious chef in the house of Jaffar. His chemistry with Tatu amazes most of their fans. According to his Instagram bio, he is also a businessman and journalist.

10. Alpha – John Wanyama

John Wanyama is popularly known by his stage name, Johny Skani. He is a singer, an events organizer, and television personality. TWanyama is the brains behind the formation of Skani Flani boy band.

At times, he hangs out with the country’s established entertainers such as Daniel Dambuki (Churchill), Shaffie Weru, Gilad Millo, Nyashsinki, Fena Githu among many others.

11. Tatu – Frere Aysher Simei

Frere Aysher Simei is a filmmaker, scriptwriter, and mother. She is also the founder of Team Kibonge. The group offers plus size women the opportunity to showcase their talents in the entertainment scene. According to her, “big is the new small.”

12. Tasha – Illya Frank

Arguably, Illya Frank is a household name in the showbiz scene. Many recognize Illya for her appearance in the Nairobi Diaries. In 2019, the actress made headlines after she revealed her white boyfriend. Some of you can remember her from the Maza series, where she features as Keisy Brown.
