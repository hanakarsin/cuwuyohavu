Actress Halima Abubakar Tackles Her Colleagues Guilty Of 'Celebrating Bad News'

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Sunday, March 17, 2024

Nollywood actress, Halima Abubakar has tackled her colleagues mourning the death of Davido’s son, Ifeanyi Adeleke.

Naija News reports that Nigerians both home and abroad are mourning the passing of the young Adeleke who died in a drowning accident in his father’s home in Banana Island.

Most celebrities, also took to social media to mourn his death, while sending their condolences to the singer and his fiance, Chioma Rowland.

Reacting to the development, Halima Abubakar slammed her colleagues who posted the photos of the late Ifeanyi on their social media pages, but did not celebrate him on his birthday while he was still alive.

She noted that such actions portrayed by some of her colleagues only prove that people celebrate sad news more than they do good news.

She further urged people to run from such people as they are not good people.

She wrote: ‘‘Half of you who posted that little baby didn’t wish him hbd. But you are the first to post his demise. Do you people think at all? It shows how many of you only celebrated news such a sad reality.. Did you wish him hbd? No!! You rush write rip. The pain is fresh. Thank God for spiritual discernment. Run from this type of people. Take control ya Rabbi.”
