12 prophesies that came true in the year 2020

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Saturday, August 3, 2024

As the year 2020, with its challenges and tragedies gradually comes to an end, Legit.ng in this report compiled some prophecies issued by revered clerics at the beginning of the year for the review of our readers.

2020 has been a tough year for Nigerians with the EndSARS protest, the coronavirus lockdown and many tragedies. Not only in Nigeria, 2020 has also been difficult for people all over the world, with millions of people contracting the novel coronavirus and thousands dying from it.

Despite all these, many people still cling to the hope of a better life, believing God's prophets.

Below are prophecies from respected men of God that came true.

Prophecies from the founder and general overseer of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries (MFM), Dr. Daniel Olukoya

1. Year where round the clock prayers are needed against national restlessness.

2. Hot prayers are required in 2020.

3. This year would be messy and confusing.

4. Year when some many leaders would be shifted away.

5. A year where all things many have planted and watered in the past years would now begin to produce fruits.

6. 2020 would be an up and down year.

Prophecy from Dr. Paul Enenche, Senior Pastor of Dunamis International Gospel Centre that came true:

1. 2020, what will happen in this time in our country Nigeria will not take time, it will happen like a dream. It shall be drastic, dramatic and dimensional in nature.

Prophecies from Father Ejike Mbaka, leader of Adoration Ministry, Enugu, Nigeria (AMEN), that came true:

1. Many things are going to happen in Nigeria this 2020 that will shock countrymen and countrywomen, but all would be to God’s glory.

2. In spite of all that would happen this 2020, there is hope. In Imo state, there is hope. Hope, hope, hope … hope in Imo state! Imo people have suffered (but) God is raising a new hope that would be an agent of salvation for them.

Prophecies from Primate Babatunde Elijah Ayodele, Founder of INRI Evangelical Spiritual Church, Oke-Afa, Ejigbo Lagos, that came true:

1. Nigeria 2020 needs to pray so well because I foresee so many things happening in the country. The country will face serious economic challenges.

2. Despite all this, there will still be security challenges in major parts of the country.

3. Let us pray to rebuke the death of a present and former Minister, former governor, and Former president in Nigeria.

Meanwhile, Aanu Adegun, a journalist from Lagos had earlier compiled some prophesies any Nigerian can make that will somehow come to pass in 2020.

For many pastors, prophets and prophetesses, the beginning of every year is the time God speaks to the people through them.

It is the time men of God reel out prophesies upon prophecies. Though many people have argued that these prophecies are mere predictions from these acclaimed men of God, this has not stopped them from churning out more and more prophesies every blessed year.
