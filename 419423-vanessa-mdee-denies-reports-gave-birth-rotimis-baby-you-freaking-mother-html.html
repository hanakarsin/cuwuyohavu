Vanessa Mdee Denies Reports She Gave Birth to Rotimi's Baby: &quot;You're Freaking Out My Mother&quot;

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Friday, June 7, 2024
  • Tanzanian songstress Vanessa Mdee dismissed reports that she gave birth
  • Via Instagram, Mdee said that the unfounded claims had shocked her mother
  • The singer who is dating actor Rotimi wondered who had started the rumour

Tanzanian songstress Vanessa Mdee has dismissed claims that she gave birth to her actor fiancé Rotimi's child.

Via a message on her Instagram stories, the former East Africa Got Talent judge said the false reports freaked out her mother.

According to Mdee, her mother had gone to visit her relatives in Arusha, Tanzania, when she heard about her daughter's alleged delivery.

Mdee further sought to find out from her followers who started the rumour that she had made the Coming to America 2 actor a father.
