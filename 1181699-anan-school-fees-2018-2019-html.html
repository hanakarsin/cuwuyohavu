ANAN school fees 2018-2019 - Legit.ng

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Monday, July 29, 2024

ANAN school fees are a matter of interest for all the prospective students of this educational facility. The fees for the upcoming 2018/2019 school season are already known. Below, you will find all the available information on ANAN fees.

ANAN fees for 2018-2019

First, one of the payments that’s of the biggest interest for most prospective students of the Association of National Accountants of Nigeria is the ANAN induction fee which is N214,000. Another important payment for prospective accounting students is the cost of membership registration, which is N48,000.

Now, let’s get down to all the other ANAN fees as they are for the current season.

  • To cover the development levy, a student will need to pay N20,000
  • The AIT registration in ANAN cost N15,000
  • The M.C.P.D. income is N35,000
  • The NCA, development levy cost N10,000
  • The induction fee, as it was mentioned cost N214,000
  • The membership registration requires from students N48,000 as it has also been mentioned already
  • Fellowship conferment is another payment, and it cost N75,000
  • The cost of practicing stamps, etc. is N10, the smallest payment on the list!
  • The annual conference cost N45,000
  • The annual subscription is N15,000
  • Practitioners forum fees cost N25,000
  • Life insurance will is N3,000
  • The license renewal practice is N65,000

  • Practicing forms and insurance policies are free of charge!

How to pay ANAN school fees

You can visit the official website of the facility and see the online payment options, which are available there. You will see a list of fees for you to pay, just like this one. You can always check out any payment and required information on this website.

Provide the required information in the fields to the left from the list. Then, you need to submit the operation and that’s it! You have paid everything! Don’t forget to print out all the slips and bills.
