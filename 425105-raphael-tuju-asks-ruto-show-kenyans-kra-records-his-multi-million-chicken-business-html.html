Raphael Tuju Asks Ruto to Show Kenyans KRA Records from His Multi-Million Chicken Business

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Monday, July 8, 2024
  • Jubilee Party secretary-general Raphael Tuju dared William Ruto to show Kenyans his proof of tax payments from his chicken business
  • Tuju sarcastically thanked the DP for revealing how much he makes because his monetary donations were not adding up
  • The SG said as a Bible-quoting Christian, the deputy president should show Kenyans his tax payments from his mega chicken enterprise

Jubilee Party secretary-general Raphael Tuju has dared Deputy President William Ruto to show Kenyans his tax payments to the Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA) from his multi-million chicken business.

Tuju was speaking in reference to a damning revelation by the DP in which he said he gets KSh 1.5 million daily from his poultry business in Sugoi.

In a media briefing on Monday, September 6, the tough-talking SG said just like Ruto was transparent enough to share information about his humongous earnings, he should likewise, show his tax records.

"We congratulate DP Ruto for his chicken farm that makes Sh1.5 million every day. We also request him to show the big payments he made to the KRA. Give unto Caesar what belongs to Caesar," he said.

The SG sarcastically thanked Ruto for disclosing how much he makes from his chicken business because the money paid to his many visitors in Karen was questionable

"People of goodwill may have thought of making a contribution (to Ruto) but with an income of KSh 1.5 million, the deputy president is okay," he added.

Tuju said Ruto, a Bible-quoting Christian, should "shame the devil" and give all Kenyans his KRA records from his multi-million business as a leader should lead from the front.

Attacking Kenyatta family

At the same time, Tuju also demanded that Ruto tames his allies and bloggers who have been attacking late former president Jomo Kenyatta and his family.

"As a party, we are sending the DP a public message that we will not countenance his venturing into crossing this line. He must call all his goons to order," he said.

He also sarcastically hailed Ruto for amassing choppers, hotels and other assets in a short period of time adding that the DP should show hustlers how to accumulate such wealth.
