Who has the most followers on Instagram in the world?

Without a doubt, social media’s infiltration into the lives of internet users has been on the rise. The most recent figures of individuals on these platforms, especially Instagram, is fascinating.

Whether you are on Instagram to share your images and great things about your life or are purely business-oriented, you would agree that accumulating followers is one of your most extraordinary agendas. Some have attracted the entire world's attention and have numerous followers, maybe because of their popularity or exciting content.

1. Cristiano Ronaldo (@cristiano) – 272 million

Cristiano Ronaldo, the living football legend, has the highest Instagram followers with over 272 million fans. Considering his exceptional football abilities and impressive performance on the pitch, it is no surprise that he has accumulated an enormous following over time.

The Portugal and Juventus talisman became the first person worldwide to reach 200 million followers on Instagram. It seems he is not only breaking records on the pitch but online as well. Cristiano is often described as "the phenomenal phenomenon" by fans because of his tremendous influence.
