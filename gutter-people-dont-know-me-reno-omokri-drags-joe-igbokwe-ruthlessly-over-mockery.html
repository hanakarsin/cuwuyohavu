'Gutter People Dont Know Me'

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Wednesday, May 1, 2024

Former presidential aide, Reno Omokri, has berated the Lagos-based All Progressives Congress, APC chieftain, Joe Igbokwe.

This comes after the APC chieftain took to his Facebook to question who the controversial author, Reno Omokri was, adding that he did not know who is or what he stands for.

He wrote; “Who is Reno Omokri

“Honestly nobody knows him or what he stands for… I do not know his antecedents, his life, his story, his pedigree, his disposition, his education, his family, his work experience, his achievements, where he is coming from, Honestly i do not know where to place him.”

Reacting to the shade, Reno via his Instagram slammed Igbokwe.

Sharing photos of himself with the past and present Prime Minister of the United Kingdom, he added that he’s not surprised that ‘Gutter people’ do not know who he is.

He wrote: “Dear Joe Igbokwe; Thank you for your comments. If I am not mistaken, you are the Special Adviser to the Lagos state Governor for Gutters and Drainages.

“Please find attached a photo of me with the incumbent Prime Minister of the United Kingdom, and another with his immediate predecessor, Theresa May, taken while she was in office.

“You say you don’t know me. I am not surprised. GUTTER people won’t know me. Only GREATER people do!

“Thank you and may God bless you.”
